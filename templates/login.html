Discover Tucson on two wheels with Tugo Bike Share! With over 330 bikes and 41 stations, navigating the city has never been easier or more fun!

HOW IT WORKS

Purchase a pass online, with PBSC app or at one of out kiosks. Locate a bitke at one of our 41 stations across the city to unlock and get ride

Ride for as long as you like with the Pay-As-You-Go Pass - $1 to unlock + 50.25 per minute of ride time. For Monthly, Annual and Tugo-For-All Pass holders, after 30 minutes, overage fees apply

Return the bike to any station citywide. Slide the bike firmly into an empty dock, wait for the green light to confirm the bike is docked and the trip will close.
